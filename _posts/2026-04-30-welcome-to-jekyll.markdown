Over the last few years, we have worked to build a program
that would properly prepare students for a career in computer
science. Our coding pathway contains four courses:
1. Computer Science: Foundations
2. Honors Coding I
3. AP Computer Science A
4. Honors Coding Practicum

In addition, we have added a Cybersecurity I class (which can be taken
anytime after Computer Science: Foundations).

Throughout these courses, students complete hands-on projects,
often of their own design, 
ranging from getting a rectangle to appear on the screen to
fully-fledged 3D video games built in Unity. One goal of this
website is to highlight some of the impressive projects that students
create. We find them inspiring, and we hope that you do too!
